PSTN Dialers

Elmax srl, established in 2000th, is a dynamic and young Italian manufacturer of electronic solutions for security in residential and commercial buildings. Our products are alarm control panels for intrusion detection, auto diallers GSM and PSTN, integrated in control panels or stand alone, gates opener and all the accessories as electronic keys, keypad and radio receivers. For us security is not an abstract concept but something very concrete. For this reason ELMAX is a constantly searching for new solutions and innovations in a market that requires standards, performance and high quality without forgetting, however, easy of installation and use. In the last time we have released also domotic apps – iOS and Android platforms – for the remote control of our systems. Finally, most of our systems are programmable by simple voice prompts.
