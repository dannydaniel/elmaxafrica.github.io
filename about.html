Company Profile

Elmax srl, established in 2000th, is a dynamic and young Italian manufacturer of electronic solutions for security in residential and commercial buildings. Our products are alarm control panels for intrusion detection, auto diallers GSM and PSTN, integrated in control panels or stand alone, gates opener and all the accessories as electronic keys, keypad and radio receivers. For us security is not an abstract concept but something very concrete. For this reason ELMAX is a constantly searching for new solutions and innovations in a market that requires standards, performance and high quality without forgetting, however, easy of installation and use. In the last time we have released also domotic apps – iOS and Android platforms – for the remote control of our systems. Finally, most of our systems are programmable by simple voice prompts.

One of our strengths is the R&D department, very active to study the technological innovations with a team of technicians and engineers who put all their expertise to design of new products with the help of advanced instruments.

A CAD department for design and construction of prototypes. It represents the first step in the industrialization of the product, including all documentation required for the production, such as list of materials, mechanical drawings and technical manuals.

An engineering department – hardware and firmware – for the development of all the microchip boards. The firmware is developed using advanced instruments such as emulators, simulators and digital oscilloscopes.

A production department with qualified staff performs – with the utmost care and thoroughness – final assembling and testing to ensure every product follow the required standards in terms of quality and functionality.

The satisfaction of our customers is our satisfaction. In Elmax there will always be someone who can help our customers to solve technical problems. Our goal is to keep improving the relationship with our clients, bringing our expertise and our experience closer and closer. All our production is made in Italy, certified, with particular attention to aesthetic design.
